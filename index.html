What's Holistic Theraphy?

If you are here, you might know what Holistic Theraphy is and its concerns,
However, here is an explanation and some benefits just in case :)

In its most basic form, Holistic Therapy addresses the mind, body, and spirit to support health and healing.
It's very similar to general therapy or counseling but often draws on complementary and alternative practices that the therapist may also have in their toolbox.

How can Holistic Theraphy help you?

  • Stress Reduction and Healthy Coping Skills
  • Spiritual Connection
  • Relapse Prevention
  • Better Overall Health
  • Increased Self Esteem and Identity
